FAQs for TCL MOVE智能手环

My TCL MOVE智能手环 doesn’t seem to charge

Use the TCL approved USB cable. If the TCL MOVE智能手环 still doesn’t charge, try a different USB cable and connect it to the USB port of a computer. You can also try connecting the charger to a different power source.

I can’t set up my TCL MOVE智能手环 to work with my phone

  • Make sure that your TCL MOVE智能手环 is charged.
  • Make sure that the software version of your Android device is Android 4.3 or later or your IOS device is IOS 8.0 or later, and supports Bluetooth version 4.2.
  • Make sure that you have installed the latest version of the TCL智能手环 app on your phone, and then pair and connect your band with your phone.
  • Make sure that your TCL MOVE智能手环 is within a 10-metre range of your phone.
  • Make sure that the Bluetooth function of your phone is turned on.
  • Turn off and then turn on the Bluetooth function on your phone and wait for a minute.
  • Set up your TCL MOVE智能手环 again to work with your phone.
  • If there is still no connection, reset or unpair your band and try to reconnect.

The data from the TCL MOVE智能手环 looks incorrect

  • Make sure that you wear TCL MOVE智能手环 on your dominant wrist when collecting data, and make sure that you sleep at least one night wearing your TCL MOVE智能手环 before attempting to get a measurement.
  • Make sure you have an active internet connection to ensure successful synchronisation of data between your TCL MOVE智能手环 and the app on your phone.

How to reset your TCL MOVE智能手环

  • On your phone, find and touch TCL MOVE智能手环 > Reset TCL MOVE智能手环
  • Touch “Erase all data and reset”.

What can I do if my TCL MOVE智能手环 can’t receive notifications?

To enable your TCL MOVE智能手环 to receive notifications from smartphone, follow the steps below.

  1. Enable the TCL智能手环 app to start automatically once the phone is powered on.
  2. Add the TCL智能手环 app to the whitelist or protected app list.
  3. Turn off any power saving settings which disable the app when the screen is locked.
  4. Go to Settings > Notifications > Notifications access > TCL智能手环. Disable TCL智能手环 notifications, then enable them again.
  5. Restart your smartphone.
